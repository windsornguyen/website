Today is the one year anniversary of Dedalus Labs.

On May 5th, 2025, **Dedalus Labs, Inc.** official incorporated as a Delaware C corp company, exactly 365 days ago.

My co-founder [Cathy](https://x.com/itsCathyDi) and I founded the company in her Princeton dorm room. It's remarkable looking back on it.

We were so excited to found a company together. We incorporated the company even before applying to [Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com). We were confident that things would work out, even if an accelerator didn't take us.

Fortunately, we got into [Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com) a few days later.
